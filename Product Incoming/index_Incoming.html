Recraftle Ashtray

Elevate your home decor with this stunning piece,
a testament to the power of upcycling. Crafted from
solid concrete and a captivating blend of recycled
materials, including cigarette butts, crushed glass,
and torn pages from vintage books, this unique creation
is a conversation starter and a symbol of sustainability.

The contrasting textures and colors create a visually
striking piece that adds depth and character to any space.
The cigarette butts, once discarded, are transformed into a
mesmerizing mosaic, while the crushed glass and torn pages
evoke a sense of history and storytelling.

This home decor piece is not just a beautiful addition to
your space; it's also a reminder of the importance of reducing
waste and repurposing materials. It's a testament to the creativity
and resourcefulness that can emerge from unexpected sources.
Embrace the power of transformation and make a statement with
this unique and eco-friendly home decor piece.
